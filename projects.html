Exam Authorisation

This System is a secure online exam authorization system designed specifically for Richfield College's IT assessments. Traditionally, contact students required a password entered by an invigilator to access each online assessment. This Webapp replaces that manual password entry with a streamlined QR code-based process.

When a student attempts to take an assessment, a unique QR code is generated including their full name, the assessment details, and a secure signature to validate it's authenticity. An invigilator, after authenticating with their organizational Microsoft account, scans the QR code to authorize the student. This process securely logs the student's and invigilator's details along with a timestamp in the database, tying the authorization event to both parties and the specific assessment. By automating and securing the authorization process, this system prevents unauthorized access, simplifies exam management, and adds a layer of accountability for both students and exam staff.

Technologies: HTML5, CSS3, TailwindCSS, JavaScript, Node.js, Neon PostgreSQL.
